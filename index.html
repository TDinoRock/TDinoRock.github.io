Accurate Weaponry

keyboard_arrow_down

Accurate Weaponry is a physics-based, open-world VR game that redefines medieval combat with a focus on realistic technique, strength, and endurance. I learned a lot about VR technology and tracking as well as the difficulty to replicate real-world physics we interact with every day. I also learned how to program AI to be responsive to specific situations and control their difficulty. I had a lot of fun researching every weapon's techniques and creating training exercises to help the player learn how to use them.


Made in Unity in C# language with Unity XR Rig then moved to Meta SDK Rig
