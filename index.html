Accurate Weaponry

My name is Trennon Talbot, and I am a Sophomore year student at Boise State University. My major is in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology's, or GIMM for short. Now that introductions are out of the way, this is my portfolio website showcasing my various programming projects from 2D platformer to VR open world. My most recent work has been developing "Accurate Weaponry," a highly physics-based, open world VR where players learn how to use different Medieval weapons. This VR game is not just a training simulation, but also a story, a world builder, and eventually, an MMORPG.

Other recent projects include "Marine Training Simulator" and "Brain Rot."
