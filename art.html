First Rigging - Tip Toe

keyboard_arrow_down

This is the first rigging animation I created while learning Autodesk Maya. I learned the basics of rigging a character and animating simple movements. I had fun seeing the character come to life as it tip toed across the ground. Even though its not a full walk cycle on a more complex character, it was a great start to learning rigging and animation.

First 22 Years

keyboard_arrow_down

First 22 Years is a rotoscoped video of the life of a female human in their first 22 years. This video took many hours to edit because I did not properly film each scene with a green screen background which would have made rotoscoping easier. I learned a lot about the importance of proper film techniques and a change in workflow. I had created the scene first and then filmed the actors rather than the other way. It was fun to film each scene with the help of my friends and family, and then to see the final product.


Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Blender

3D Background For Animation

keyboard_arrow_down

This is the 3D background I created for my First 22 Years animation. I learned a lot about when to do certain tasks in the workflow. I had initially created the background in Photoshop before I had filmed the scenes for the animation. Then I imported the background into Blender to create a 3D environment. In hindsight, I should have created the entire background in Blender. I had fun learning how to use Blender to create a 3D environment.


Adobe Photoshop and Blender

keyboard_arrow_down
