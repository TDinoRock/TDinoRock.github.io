First 22 Years

keyboard_arrow_down

First 22 Years is a rotoscoped video of the life of a female human in their first 22 years. This video took many hours to edit because I did not properly film each scene to make the rotoscoping easier. I learned a lot about the importance of proper film techniques and a change in workflow. I had created a scene then filmed rather than the other way. I had fun when we filmed each scene with the help of my friends and family, and then to see the final product.


Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Blender
